# Harborview Health Insurance — Plan Overview

Harborview Health Insurance offers four individual health plan tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. All plans include access to our nationwide provider network and 24/7 telehealth.

## Bronze Plan
- Monthly premium: $280
- Annual deductible: $6,500 (individual) / $13,000 (family)
- Coinsurance after deductible: 40%
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $8,700 (individual)
- Best for: people who want low premiums and rarely visit the doctor

## Silver Plan
- Monthly premium: $410
- Annual deductible: $3,500 (individual) / $7,000 (family)
- Coinsurance after deductible: 30%
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $7,200 (individual)
- Best for: people who want a balance between premium cost and coverage

## Gold Plan
- Monthly premium: $560
- Annual deductible: $1,200 (individual) / $2,400 (family)
- Coinsurance after deductible: 20%
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $6,000 (individual)
- Best for: people who see doctors regularly or manage a chronic condition

## Platinum Plan
- Monthly premium: $720
- Annual deductible: $250 (individual) / $500 (family)
- Coinsurance after deductible: 10%
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $4,500 (individual)
- Best for: people who want the most predictable costs and the lowest deductible

All plans include three free primary care visits per year before the deductible applies, and free annual preventive screenings as required by law.
