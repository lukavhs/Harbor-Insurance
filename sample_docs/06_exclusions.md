# What's Not Covered

While Harborview Health Insurance plans cover a wide range of medical care, the following are generally excluded from all plan tiers:

- Cosmetic procedures that are not medically necessary (e.g., elective cosmetic surgery, teeth whitening)
- Experimental or investigational treatments not approved by the FDA, except where required by law for clinical trial participation
- Weight-loss surgery or treatments, unless deemed medically necessary and pre-authorized for a qualifying diagnosis
- Long-term custodial care, such as ongoing nursing home stays for non-medical assistance with daily living
- Acupuncture, massage therapy, and other alternative treatments, unless purchased as part of a separate wellness add-on
- Services received outside of the country, except for emergency care as defined in your plan documents
- Personal comfort items during a hospital stay, such as televisions or private rooms requested without medical necessity
- Fertility treatments such as IVF, unless covered under a separate fertility benefit rider purchased in addition to the base plan

If you're unsure whether a specific treatment is covered, contact member services before receiving care, or ask your provider's office to submit a prior authorization request so Harborview can confirm coverage in advance.

These exclusions are general guidelines for this demo and don't reflect the exclusions of any real insurance policy.
