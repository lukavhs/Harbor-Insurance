# Covered Services

The following services are covered under all Harborview Health Insurance plans, subject to each plan's deductible and coinsurance unless noted otherwise.

## Preventive care (covered at 100%, no deductible)
- Annual wellness exams
- Routine vaccinations
- Standard cancer screenings (mammograms, colonoscopies after age 45, etc.)
- Well-child visits

## Doctor visits
- Primary care visit: $30 copay (Gold and Platinum), subject to deductible on Bronze and Silver
- Specialist visit: $60 copay (Gold and Platinum), subject to deductible on Bronze and Silver
- Telehealth visit: $15 copay on all plans

## Emergency and urgent care
- Urgent care visit: $75 copay
- Emergency room visit: $350 copay, then coinsurance applies
- Ambulance transport: covered at plan coinsurance rate after deductible

## Prescription drugs
Drugs are grouped into four tiers:
- Tier 1 (generic): $10 copay
- Tier 2 (preferred brand): $35 copay
- Tier 3 (non-preferred brand): $70 copay
- Tier 4 (specialty): 25% coinsurance, capped at $250 per fill

## Mental health and substance use
- Outpatient therapy: same copay as a specialist visit
- Inpatient mental health treatment: covered at plan coinsurance rate after deductible
- No separate annual visit limit — mental health benefits are matched to medical benefits as required by federal law

## Maternity and newborn care
- Prenatal visits, delivery, and postnatal care are covered at the plan's coinsurance rate after deductible
- Newborns are automatically covered for the first 30 days; a separate enrollment form must be submitted to continue coverage afterward

## Major procedures
- Surgeries, hospital stays, and major procedures (including joint replacements) are covered at the plan's coinsurance rate after the deductible is met
- Prior authorization is required for most non-emergency surgeries and all inpatient stays
